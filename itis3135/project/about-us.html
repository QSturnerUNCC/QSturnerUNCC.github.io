About Us

Our History and Mission

We were started in 2021 when we saw a problem that needed to be fixed. If you are a pet owner, which we are assuming you are, you know how much STINK your pet creates. So we thought,

"This problem doesn't need to exist!!"

We then got to work, determined to solve this problem. Months later (in October 2021), we arrived at our solution: P.H.A.T. Tags! Each P.H.A.T. Tag is specially designed to keep your pet smelling fresh, even if you haven't bathed them in months! With scents that are 100% healthy for your pet, you can wish that STINK farewell! (Read more)
